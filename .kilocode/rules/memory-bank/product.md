# GridGuide - Product Definition

## Purpose
GridGuide is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to provide Electric Vehicle (EV) owners with a rapid, accurate, and estimated cost for charging their vehicle based on user-defined parameters. The app eliminates cost anxiety and simplifies trip planning for EV owners.

## Problems Solved
- Eliminates guesswork about charging session costs
- Provides precise financial estimates before plugging in
- Helps with trip planning and budgeting for EV travel
- Simplifies the calculation of energy required versus station pricing

## Target Users
- Electric Vehicle owners
- EV commuters and travelers
- Anyone looking to optimize their charging habits and costs

## How It Works
1. User inputs current battery percentage
2. User inputs target battery percentage
3. User specifies total battery capacity in kWh
4. User enters cost per kWh at charging station
5. App calculates energy needed and estimated cost
6. Results displayed with financial estimate and planned range addition

## User Experience Goals
- Minimal input friction with intuitive controls
- Mobile-first design optimized for on-the-go use
- Clean, modern interface with clear visual feedback
- Fast calculations with immediate results
- Dark-mode design for excellent visibility in all conditions
